Romer v. Evans

Foubister, Murray. Colorado Ski Areas. Jpg file, 12 Nov. 2012.

Romer v. Evans opened the door for sweeping changes in the United States and was the start of widespread support from the Supreme Court, as well as other state governments. Now, the veracity of the past precedent of Bowers v. Hardwick was put into question.

Amendment 2 inspired many organizations, like the Citizens Project, a nonprofit that is still advocating for equality and diversity in Colorado.

This victory for gay-rights activists set a warning to other states trying to create their own homophobic laws, after the example that Colorado set.

Romer v. Evans is one of the most important cases for gay civil rights in the United States. It was a wake-up call for activists and inspired future pro-equality legislation. Without Amendment 2’s spotlight on the inequality between homosexuals as a class of people, we might still be living in a more unequal society today.

“Amendment 2 violates the Equal Protection Clause, and the judgment of the Supreme Court of Colorado is affirmed. It is so ordered”

Justice Anthony Kennedy
